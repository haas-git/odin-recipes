Bedside Bacon

Description

Wake up to the smell of bacon with this ingenious recipe created by Micheal Scott himself.

What You Need

  • George Forman Grill
  • Bacon

Recipe

  1. Before you go to bed, set up your grill down by the foot of the bed.
  2. Lay as many strips of bacon on it as you can.
  3. Set an alarm 15 minutes earlier than you would normally wake up and go to bed.
  4. When the alarm goes off, plug in your grill and close it.
  5. 15 minutes later wake up to the smell of bacon! Disclaimer: DO NOT STEP ON YOUR GEORGE FORMAN GRILL IN EXCITEMENT. YOU CAN DIE
