Pre 5k Fettucine Alfredo

Description

This "Pre 5k Fettucine Alfredo" is the perfect carbo load before a big race

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp kosher salt
  • 1 1/2 Cups heavy cream
  • 2 Tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 9oz fresh fettucine pasta
  • 2oz parmesean cheese
  • 1/8 freshly grated nutmeg

Recipe

  1. Bring a large pot of water with 1 tablespoon of salt to a boil
  2. Add 1 cup heavy cream and the butter tot a large skillet. Melt over medium-low heat and allow mixture to reduce by one third, about 5-10 minutes.
  3. Once the sauce is reduced, turn the heat off and add remaining 1/2 cup of heavy cream and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Add pasta to the boiling water and cook according to package directions
  5. Transfer cooked pasta directoly to skillet with heavy cream
  6. Turn heat to low and add parmesean and nutmeg to pasta cooking until parmesean is melted, 1-2 minutes
