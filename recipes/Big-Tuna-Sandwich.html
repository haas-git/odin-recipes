The "Big Tuna" Sandwich

Description

This "Big Tuna" Sandwich is inspired by the big tuna, Jim Halpert. This tuna sandwich is so good your receptionist might fall in love with it.

Ingredients

  • Can of Tuna
  • 3 Tsp of mayonaise
  • 1 Celery Stick
  • 3 Tsp of relish, chopped
  • 1/2 red onion, finely diced

Recipe

  1. Open can of tuna and drain excess liquid.
  2. Dice celery and red onion into same size pieces.
  3. Combine diced celery, relish, red onion, mayo, and tuna in a large bowl.
  4. Enjoy immediately on toasted bread or store in the fridge for up to 3 days.
